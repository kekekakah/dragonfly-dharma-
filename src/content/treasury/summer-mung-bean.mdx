  <div class="article-section__content">
    As the intense heat of summer reaches its peak, the body's Yang energy is drawn to the surface, often leaving the interior vulnerable and easily agitated by summer heat (暑气). Mung bean (绿豆 *Lü Dou*) is universally recognized in classical dietary therapy as the premier ingredient for combating this seasonal extreme.

    Described as sweet and cold, mung bean specifically targets the Heart and Stomach meridians. The Heart governs the mind and spirit, which can become restless in extreme heat, while the Stomach handles digestion and fluid metabolism. By "clearing heat and resolving toxicity" (清热解毒), mung bean helps lower the body's internal temperature, soothe irritability, and promote the healthy excretion of excess fluids and metabolic waste.

    It is important to note that the skin of the mung bean contains most of its cooling properties. Therefore, traditional preparations often emphasize cooking the beans just until the skins split, capturing the clearest, most cooling essence of the broth.

    当盛夏酷暑达到顶峰时，人体的阳气浮于体表，内在反而容易变得空虚，且极易受到暑气的侵扰。在传统食养中，绿豆被公认为抵御炎夏的首选食材。

    古籍记载绿豆味甘、性寒，主要归心经与胃经。心主神明，在酷热中容易烦躁不安；胃主受纳腐熟，负责消化与水液代谢。通过“清热解毒”，绿豆能帮助降低体内温度，平息烦躁，并促进多余水分与代谢废物的排出。

    值得注意的是，绿豆的清热功效主要集中在其表皮。因此，传统的熬煮方法往往强调将绿豆煮至刚刚开花即可，这样能最大程度地保留绿豆汤清凉解暑的精华。

<div class="in-practice">
  <div class="in-practice__label">在生活中 · In Practice</div>
  <div class="in-practice__content">
    **Clear Mung Bean Broth:** For immediate relief from summer heat, boil whole mung beans in plenty of water for about 10-15 minutes, just until the water turns a clear, bright green and before the beans become mushy. Drink this clear broth at room temperature.

    **清透绿豆汤：** 若需快速消暑，可将整粒绿豆放入足量水中熬煮10-15分钟，待汤水变成清澈透亮的绿色，且绿豆尚未完全开花煮烂时关火。放凉至室温后饮用此清汤，解暑效果最佳。
